Olpegeta National Park

Ol Pejeta Conservancy, located in Kenya, is a wildlife sanctuary known for its conservation efforts and diverse animal species. It is home to endangered rhinos, including the last two northern white rhinos, and offers opportunities for game drives, safaris, and unique experiences like lion tracking.

Kisumu Impala Sanctuary

Kisumu Impala Sanctuary, located in Kenya, is a small wildlife reserve on the shores of Lake Victoria. It is home to a variety of animals, including impalas, zebras, buffaloes, and over 100 bird species. The sanctuary offers scenic views and is a peaceful destination for nature walks and wildlife viewing.
