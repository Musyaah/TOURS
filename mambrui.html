Mambrui sand dunes:

Mambrui sand dunes The Mambrui Sand Dunes are located along the northern coast of Kenya, near the town of Mambrui, just north of Malindi. These striking dunes are a natural wonder, characterized by expansive stretches of golden sand that rise dramatically against the coastal landscape. The dunes, shaped by the wind over centuries, are a popular spot for photography, adventure tourism, and sandboarding. The area offers breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean and surrounding mangrove forests. The Mambrui Sand Dunes are also known for their serene and unspoiled beauty, providing visitors with a unique blend of coastal and desert landscapes to explore.



More on Mambrui sand dunes:

The Mambrui Sand Dunes are located along the northern Kenyan coastline, near the town of Mambrui, just north of Malindi. These dramatic sand dunes, shaped by wind, rise up to 30 meters and offer stunning views of the surrounding coastline and the Indian Ocean. The dunes are a popular spot for activities such as sandboarding, hiking, and photography. Despite their arid appearance, the area is home to diverse coastal vegetation and wildlife. The Mambrui Sand Dunes are also close to attractions like the Gede Ruins and the beaches of Malindi, making it a unique destination for both adventure and relaxation.
